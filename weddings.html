Luxury Destination Weddings

Planning your dream wedding at a palace in Udaipur or a beach in Thailand? International wedding destinations are a recent trend; the thrill of an exotic location, and the beauty of the place can make your wedding just mesmerizing.
We help you design the most luxurious destination weddings, right from the mandap placement and decoration to the smaller yet important details such as the table décor, the colours, the lighting and the overall theme of the wedding no matter where in the world you’d like to have it.


Wedding Entertainment

No wedding is complete without music and merrymaking, and we’re well aware of this. Our network of entertainment artists consists of some of the best DJs, traditional musicians, live bands, emcees, dance troupes and more to create the perfect mood and atmosphere for your guests.
We also consult with you to understand the music tastes you and your guests have, the kind of entertainment you would like to offer, and the selection of a DJ who would add that energy and fun to the wedding day. Finally we connect you with the right wedding entertainment artistes who would add to the happiness of the occasion.


Wedding Photography

Capturing the right moment can help you and your spouse create a lifetime of memories to look back at and smile. Our wedding photography services include professional photography that specializes in candid/themed photography as well to suit your needs.
Celebrate tying the knot with memories that we craft in each shot and put together for the couple to cherish.

Traditional Wedding Ceremonial Arrangements

We are experienced in managing weddings from diverse communities across the country, be it North Indian weddings, South Indian weddings, Muslim weddings, Sikh weddings or Christian weddings. Traditional wedding ceremonial arrangements tend to be very elaborate and culturally diverse. Tell us your customary ways of performing ceremonial rites and rituals, and we’ll put in our best to ensure it’s done exactly the way you have traditionally always done them.

Wedding Design & Décor

Every Indian wedding is memorable for its amazing colours and festivities. We take into account the colours and elements you like and transform the wedding venue into a beautifully done up area for the wedding to take place in. We’ll create the most elaborately designed wedding mandaps, the lighting style and the overall welcome décor through our collaborations with the best floral decorators, wedding mandap decorators and wedding tent designers in Bangalore.

Wedding Videography

Video clips can capture candid moments from the wedding, creating a panorama of memories from each and every aspect and ceremony of the wedding. Our professional wedding videographers ensure that they capture all the wedding finery, the mood, the food and the entire atmosphere of the wedding to create full-length wedding videos for you and your loved ones. We also help edit these videos to craft complete stories from your wedding day.
